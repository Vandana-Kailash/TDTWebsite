Our Story

Born in the
Mountains of the Northeast

Travel Diary Tourism was founded by passionate Northeast explorers who believed that the most extraordinary stories are written on roads less traveled. We are not just a travel company — we are storytellers, mountain lovers, and culture seekers.

Specializing exclusively in Northeast India, we bring you immersive journeys to Arunachal Pradesh, Meghalaya, Sikkim, Darjeeling, and beyond — with the warmth of local hospitality and the expertise of certified mountain guides.
